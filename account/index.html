Add Refer Bonus

This functionality is used when one customer refers WorldLink's Internet package to another customer but due to some issues the NETTV account doesnt synchronize with the bonus Internet days which they earned as a part of "Refer Offer".

Note

Refer bonus adds Internet days to both the referer and the referee. The amount of days added to the referer's account depends upon the amount of people being referred.

How to Add refer bonus?

Case I: Referred account not getting the bonus

If the referred account didn't get the NETTV Refer bonus then follow these steps:

  1. Enter the Customer's username (Referred account in this case) in the USERNAME field and select the respective username from the suggestions.
  2. Choose a respective MAC Address from STB dropdown field.
  3. Related information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will be loaded after selecting the desired MAC Address. (Make sure the Is Referer checkbox is not checked)
  4. Enter the amount of bonus days that needs to get added in the Days field.
  5. Enter remarks in the Remarks field.
  6. Click the ADD button.

Case II: Both Referer and Referred account not getting the bonus

If both the referer and referred account didn't get the NETTV Refer bonus then follow these steps:

  1. Enter the Customer's username (This will be the referer account in this case) in the USERNAME field and select the respective username from the suggestions.
  2. Choose a respective MAC Address from STB dropdown field.
  3. Related information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will be loaded after selecting the desired MAC Address.
  4. Check the Is Referer checkbox.
  5. Enter the Referred Username in the Referred Username field.
  6. Enter the amount of bonus days that needs to get added in the Days field.
  7. Enter remarks in the Remarks field.
  8. Click the ADD button.

Extend Account

As the name suggest, this functionality extends the NETTV account date of the customer.

To extend account of a customer:

  1. Enter the Customer's username in the USERNAME field and select the respective username from the suggestions.
  2. Choose a respective MAC Address from STB dropdown field.
  3. Related information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will be loaded after selecting the desired MAC Address.
  4. Enter the amount of days that needs to get extended in the Days field.
  5. Enter remarks in the Remarks field.
  6. Click the ADD button.
