Account

Add Refer Bonus

Add Refer Bonus functionality is used when a customer successfully refers someone to WorldLinkâ€™s internet plan, but due to technical issues, the NETTV account doesn't synchronize with the bonus internet days they earned through the "Refer Offer".

Note

The Refer Offer grants bonus internet days to both the referrer and the referee. The referrer receives more days based on the number of successful referrals.

How to Add refer bonus?

Case I: Referred account not getting the bonus

If the referred account didn't get the NETTV Refer bonus then follow these steps:

  1. Enter the Customer's username (Referred account in this case) in the USERNAME field and select the correct username.
  2. Choose the corresponding MAC Address from STB dropdown menu.
  3. Information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will automatically populate upon selecting the MAC address. (Make sure the Is Referer checkbox is not checked)
  4. Enter the number of bonus days to be added in the Days field.
  5. Enter the remarks in the Remarks field.
  6. Click the ADD button.

Case II: Both Referer and Referred account not getting the bonus

If both the referer and referred account didn't get the NETTV Refer bonus then follow these steps:

  1. Enter the Customer's username (This will be the referer account in this case) in the USERNAME field and select the correct username.
  2. Choose the corresponding MAC Address from STB dropdown menu.
  3. Information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will automatically populate upon selecting the MAC address.
  4. Check the Is Referer checkbox.
  5. Enter the Referred customer's Username in the Referred Username field.
  6. Enter the number of bonus days to be added in the Days field.
  7. Enter the remarks in the Remarks field.
  8. Click the ADD button.

Extend Account

As the name suggest, this functionality extends a customer's NETTV account validity period.

Note

A user can only extend a Customer's account 2 times.

To extend account of a customer:

  1. Enter the Customer's username in the USERNAME field and select the correct username from the suggestions.
  2. Choose the corresponding MAC Address from STB dropdown menu.
  3. Information such as STB TYPE and MAPPING will automatically populate upon selecting the MAC address.
  4. Enter the amount of days that needs to get extended in the Days field.
  5. Enter the remarks in the Remarks field.
  6. Click the ADD button.
